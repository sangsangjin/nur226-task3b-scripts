Part 13 – Critical Thinking Questions (비판적 사고 질문)
#질문예시 답변한국어 해설
Q1 Right Dose "Right dose means that the amount of medication I give matches exactly what the doctor has prescribed. For Nasifah, the prescribed dose is 2 mg of Ondansetron, and the ampoule contains 4 mg in 2 mL, so I drew up 1 mL which is half the ampoule. I did a dose calculation during each of my three medication checks to make sure the volume is correct. This right helps prevent giving too much or too little medication, which could cause harm to the patient." 처방 용량 = 실제 투여량 일치 확인. 매 약물 확인마다 계산 검증. 과다/과소 투여 방지.
Q2 Aboriginal Patient "If Nasifah identified as Aboriginal, I would ask how she prefers to be addressed and use her preferred name throughout. I would use simple, clear language and allow extra time to explain the medication and procedure. I would ask if she would like a family member or support person present during the injection. I would also contact the hospital's Aboriginal Health Liaison Officer to provide culturally appropriate support." 선호 이름 사용, 쉬운 영어, 가족 동석 여부, Aboriginal Liaison Officer 연락.
Q3 Dosage Concern "If I was concerned about the dosage, I would not give the medication and would first recheck my drug resource to confirm the safe dose range. I would then speak to my buddy nurse or supervising nurse to get a second opinion. If the concern remains, I would contact the prescribing doctor to clarify the order. It is my responsibility as a nurse to question any order I believe may be unsafe for the patient." 투약 중단 → 약물 가이드 재확인 → 동료 간호사 상의 → 처방 의사에게 확인.
Q4 Ongoing Care "I would monitor the patient's vital signs regularly to check for any changes. I would check the injection site for any pain, swelling, or bleeding. I would also come back to check if the medication is working and if there are any side effects. Finally, I would make sure the patient is comfortable and report any concerns to the medical team." 활력징후 + 주사부위 확인 + 약효/부작용 확인 + 편안함 + 의료팀 보고.
Q5 Transgender Patient "If the patient identified as transgender, I would ask what name and pronouns they prefer and use them throughout. I would be respectful and not make any assumptions about their identity. I would give them extra privacy during the procedure, especially when exposing the injection site. I would make sure they feel safe and comfortable, and document their preferred name in the notes." 선호 이름/대명사 확인, 가정 금지, 프라이버시 보장, 안전하고 편안하게.
Q6 Self-Reflection "I think I communicated well with the patient by explaining each step and getting consent before the injection. However, I was a bit nervous so my communication could have been smoother and more natural. Next time, I will practise more so I can speak confidently while still following all the safety steps." 소통 잘함 + 긴장해서 부자연스러웠음 + 다음엔 더 연습.
Q7 Right Patient "To make sure I have the right patient, I ask them to tell me their full name and date of birth verbally. I then check their armband to confirm the name, date of birth, and URN match the medication chart. I do this twice — once when I first meet the patient and again at the bedside before giving the injection." 이름+DOB 구두 확인 → 팔찌 URN 대조 → 2회 시행.
Q8 Buddy RN Unavailable "As a student nurse, I cannot give medication without my buddy nurse, so I would wait for them to return. Even if the team leader says it is okay, I would not do it alone because it is outside my scope of practice. I would let the patient know there will be a short delay and contact my buddy nurse to find out when they will be back." 학생은 단독 투약 불가 → 대기 → 환자에게 알림 → buddy 연락.
Q9 Medication Check Steps "In a medication check, I compare the label on the medication against the chart to confirm the medication name, dose, and strength match. I also check the expiry date to make sure it is safe to use. I do this three times — first when I get the medication out, second before I draw it up, and third at the bedside with the patient." 라벨 vs 차트 (약물명/용량/강도) + 유효기간 + 3회 시행.

Quick Reference – IM 핵심 체크리스트

3 Medication Checks

  • 1st – 준비 구역
  • 2nd – 추출 후
  • 3rd – 반드시 침상에서

5 Rights x 2

  • 1st set – 2차 확인 때
  • 2nd set – 침상 3차 때
  • Patient, Medication, Dose, Route, Time

Patient ID

  • 이름 + DOB 구두 확인
  • 팔찌 URN ↔ 차트 대조
  • 총 2회 (인사 때 + 침상 때)

Allergy 3중 확인

  • 차트 알레르기 란
  • 환자에게 직접 질문
  • 빨간 알레르기 팔찌 유무

IM 핵심 (SubCut과 차이)

  • Z-track technique
  • 90도 각도, 바늘 3/4
  • 소독 스왑 필수
  • Deltoid ≤1 mL / Ventrogluteal ≤3 mL
  • 글러브 착용

Dose Calculation

  • 처방: Ondansetron 2 mg
  • 앰플: 4 mg / 2 mL
  • = 1 mL (절반만 추출)
  • 나머지 → 폐기
