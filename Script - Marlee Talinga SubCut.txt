(한국어: 주사 시행 전 손위생.)

1. SWAB THE SITE:
"I will clean the injection site with an antiseptic swab

(소독 스왑으로 주사 부위를 바깥쪽으로 한 번 닦고 건조 대기)

"I am going to pinch the skin to create a fold of

(피하 조직 접히도록 피부를 집는다)

4. INJECT MEDICATION:
"I am now injecting the medication slowly."
(약물을 천천히 주입)

   *** NOTE: Do NOT aspirate for subcutaneous heparin. ***
   (중요: 피하 헤파린은 흡인(aspirate)하지 않는다.)

5. REMOVE NEEDLE:
"I am removing the needle smoothly."
(바늘을 부드럽게 제거)

   *** NOTE: Do NOT massage the site after heparin injection
       – this can increase bruising. ***
   (중요: 헤파린 주사 후 주사부위를 문지르지 않는다 – 멍 악화 방지.)

(바늘과 주사기를 주사침 폐기통에 바로 버린다)

Q1: Self-Reflection (자가 성찰)
    "Reflecting on your administration, is there anything
     important you would consider or do differently next time?"
----------------------------------------------------------------------

SAMPLE ANSWER:

"Reflecting on my administration, I think there are a few
things I could improve for next time."

"First, I could have been more thorough with my hand hygiene.
I should make sure I am doing hand hygiene at every required
moment – before touching the patient, before the procedure,
after touching the patient, and after touching the patient's
surroundings."

"Second, I think I could have explained the medication and
its purpose more clearly to the patient. Next time, I would
take a bit more time to make sure the patient fully understands
why they are receiving the injection and what to expect."

"Third, I should also check the expiry dates of all my
equipment, not just the medication – for example, the syringe,
the needles, and the alcohol swab."

(한국어: 자가 성찰 예시 답변
 1) 손위생을 더 철저히 해야 했다
 2) 약물 설명을 더 명확히 해야 했다
 3) 약물뿐 아니라 장비(주사기, 바늘, 스왑)의 유효기간도 확인해야 했다

----------------------------------------------------------------------
Q2: Ongoing Care / Assessment (지속 관리 및 평가)
    "What ongoing care or assessments might you provide
     to Marlee in the coming hours?"
----------------------------------------------------------------------

SAMPLE ANSWER:

"For Marlee's ongoing care, I would do the following."

"First, I would continue to monitor her vital signs regularly,
including her temperature, heart rate, blood pressure, and
respiratory rate, because she is postoperative and at risk
of complications."

"Second, I would check the injection site for any signs of
excessive bruising or bleeding, since heparin can increase
bleeding risk."

"Third, I would monitor for signs of wound infection at her
surgical site on the tibia. I would check for redness, swelling,
warmth, and any discharge around the wound dressing."

"Fourth, I would do a pain assessment to make sure her pain
is well managed after surgery."

"Fifth, I would encourage Marlee to mobilise as able,
and make sure her TED stockings are fitted properly, as
these are also part of her VTE prevention plan."

"Sixth, I would monitor her platelet count if she continues
on heparin, to watch for heparin-induced thrombocytopenia."

(한국어: 지속 관리 답변 예시
 1) 활력징후 지속 모니터링 (수술 후 합병증 위험)
 2) 주사부위 멍/출혈 확인 (헤파린 부작용)
 3) 수술 상처 감염 징후 관찰 (발적, 부종, 열감, 분비물)
 4) 통증 사정
 5) 조기 보행 격려 + TED 스타킹 착용 확인 (VTE 예방)
 6) 혈소판 수치 모니터링 (HIT 감시))

   - Ask patient directly (환자에게 직접 질문)
